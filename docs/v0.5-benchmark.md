### Ear-angle residual σ

| | v0.4 | v0.5 |
|---|---|---|
| Left ear σ | 4.06° | **3.66°** |
| Right ear σ | 4.09° | 4.30° |

Left ear improved 10% (4.06° → 3.66°). Right ear regressed slightly (4.09° → 4.30°). Overall improvement is asymmetrical — the additional video primarily tightened left-ear placement on this clip.

## What this version shows

v0.5 is the first version past the v0.4 plateau. The left ear continues to improve, but the right ear does not. This is consistent with the diminishing-returns pattern: after ~400 instances, per-keypoint improvements come from scene coverage of specific failure modes, not raw instance count.
