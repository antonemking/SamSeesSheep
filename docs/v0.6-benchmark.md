## A documented regression

v0.6 is a split result. Right ear σ hits an all-time best (3.55°, surpassing v0.4's 4.09° and v0.5's 4.30°). But left ear σ regresses to 4.65° — worse than v0.3 (4.82° on the same clip in the 3-way, though note the different methodology) and effectively back to near-v0.2 territory.

This is the clearest evidence that more data does not monotonically improve every keypoint. Per-keypoint labeling consistency and per-session coverage of specific failure modes matter at least as much as total instance count. The additional training video introduced variation that helped right-ear placement but degraded left-ear placement on this particular held-out clip.

The regression is published deliberately. A measurement methodology that only reports improvements is not a methodology — it's marketing.
