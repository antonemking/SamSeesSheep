Methodology
· Segmentation: facebook/sam3 (text-prompted video tracker, "sheep head" + "sheep ear")
· Ear angle: PCA on head mask for the dorsal axis; ear direction from base→tip
· Thresholds: McLennan & Mahmoud 2019 (SPFES), Reefmann et al. 2009, Boissy et al. 2011
· Feasibility study (N=5 sheep, one homestead) — see VALIDATION.md for claim boundaries
