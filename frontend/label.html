Keypoint labeler

5 points per sheep head. Drag a dot onto the anatomy (sets v=2). If a keypoint is occluded or out-of-frame, leave it dashed at bbox center (v=0) — the exporter will skip it and training learns from the visible keypoints only. Do not guess positions for invisible keypoints.
L / R are image-space: "L-base" = the ear on the left side of the screen in this frame, not the animal's anatomical left. When the sheep turns around, the physical ear behind each slot changes — that's correct.
