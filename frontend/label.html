Keypoint labeler

5 points per sheep. Drag a dot onto the anatomy (sets v=2). If a keypoint is occluded or out-of-frame, leave it dashed at bbox center (v=0) — training skips it and learns from the visible keypoints only. Do not guess positions for invisible keypoints.
Multi-subject: each sheep is a separate instance under the same video_id. Click a sheep's bbox or a dimmed dot to make that sheep active — active sheep's 5 dots are draggable, others render dimmed. The pills above the frame let you switch too.
Nose: tip of the muzzle (most forward-projecting point, where the nostrils are). Ear base: where ear meets skull. Ear tip: distal-most point.
L / R are image-space: "L-base" = ear on the left side of the screen in this frame, not the animal's anatomical left.
